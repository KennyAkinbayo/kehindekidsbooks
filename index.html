About the Author

Kehinde Akinola creates fun, educational coloring books that spark imagination in kids ages 4–8. Each page blends creativity with simple, kid‑friendly designs that parents and teachers love.

When he isn’t designing new books, Kehinde enjoys coding, exploring nature with his family, and finding playful ways to make learning exciting.
